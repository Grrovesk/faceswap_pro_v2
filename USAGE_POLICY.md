# Usage Policy

This software performs face-swap and lip-sync operations on video.
The same tools that produce legitimate creative work can also produce
non-consensual sexual imagery, political disinformation, identity
theft, and harassment material. The maintainers take that seriously.

By using this software, you agree to the following terms in addition
to the Apache 2.0 license:

## Prohibited uses

You may NOT use this software to:

1. **Produce sexually explicit content** depicting any identifiable
   person without their explicit, documented, and informed consent.
2. **Produce CSAM** (child sexual abuse material), of real or
   AI-generated minors, in any form, for any reason.
3. **Defraud, deceive, or impersonate** real people in financial,
   legal, journalistic, political, or romantic contexts where the
   viewer would reasonably believe the depicted person is the real
   person speaking or acting.
4. **Spread disinformation** about identifiable public or private
   figures — politicians, executives, journalists, or any individual.
5. **Harass, intimidate, defame, or sexualize** any person.
6. **Circumvent voice/likeness consent laws** in your jurisdiction.
7. **Train surveillance, biometric identification, or
   law-enforcement-targeting models** on the outputs of this software
   without independent legal authorization.

## Required practices

You agree to:

1. **Obtain consent** from any identifiable person whose face appears
   in your inputs OR outputs, in writing if the output will be
   published, before publication.
2. **Disclose synthesis** when publishing outputs intended for any
   audience that might mistake the result for an unedited recording.
   Use visible watermarks, captions, or metadata tags
   (e.g. `C2PA` content credentials) as appropriate to the medium.
3. **Comply with local law** including but not limited to: the EU AI
   Act, the U.S. NO FAKES Act and state-level deepfake statutes,
   GDPR / CCPA / PIPEDA / similar privacy regimes, copyright and
   right-of-publicity law.
4. **Honor takedown requests** from depicted individuals promptly and
   in good faith.
5. **Use safety controls**. When deploying this software as a service
   to third parties, you must implement consent verification,
   watermarking, and abuse reporting.

## Enforcement

The maintainers reserve the right to:

- Refuse to merge contributions that weaken safety controls or
  remove watermarks.
- Refuse support requests for prohibited uses.
- Report illegal uses (CSAM, fraud) to law enforcement.

## Reporting abuse

If you discover this software being used in violation of this policy,
please open a GitHub issue marked `abuse-report` or contact the
maintainers directly. We will investigate in good faith.

## Acknowledgement

Face-swap and lip-sync technology is fundamentally dual-use. We
release this software believing that the creative, educational,
accessibility, and entertainment uses substantially outweigh the
harms when reasonable safeguards are in place. We rely on the
community of users and contributors to maintain those safeguards.

If you cannot agree to this policy, do not use this software.
